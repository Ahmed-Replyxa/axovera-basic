Frequently Asked Questions
What is a Fragility Score? +
The Fragility Score (0–100) measures how structurally vulnerable your ad system is. A score of 0–34 is Safe — your system has healthy profit margins and can absorb CPC increases. A score of 35–64 is a Warning — you're profitable but one bad week could flip you negative. A score of 65–100 is Collapse Risk — your system is either already losing money or one small shock away from it.
What is Break-Even CPC? +
Break-Even CPC is the maximum you can pay per click before your campaign stops making money. It's calculated as: Net Margin × Conversion Rate. If your actual CPC is above this number, you're losing money on every click — even if your ROAS looks okay.
What does CPC Headroom mean? +
CPC Headroom tells you how much your Cost Per Click can rise before you hit break-even. For example, a 40% headroom means your CPC can increase by 40% and you'll still be profitable. The lower this number, the more fragile your system is to platform price increases or increased competition.
What is the Volatility Shock simulation? +
The Volatility Shock sliders let you simulate worst-case market conditions — like a CPC spike during peak season or a CVR drop from ad fatigue. It shows you exactly what your profit per click would be under those conditions, so you know in advance whether your system can survive.
How is Axovera different from a ROAS calculator? +
ROAS calculators tell you what happened. Axovera tells you what's about to happen. We analyze the structural integrity of your ad system — stress-testing it across 9 scenarios to find hidden collapse risks that standard metrics completely miss. A 3x ROAS can still be a collapsing system. Axovera shows you why.
