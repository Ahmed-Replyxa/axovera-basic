Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

At Axovera, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Axovera, we may collect the following information:

  • Usage data — The numbers you enter into the tool (AOV, CVR, COGS, CPC) are processed locally in your browser and are not stored on our servers.
  • Purchase information — When you purchase Axovera Pro, our payment processor (Paddle) collects your email address and payment details. We receive your email address to deliver your license key.
  • Analytics data — We may collect anonymous usage data such as page visits and feature usage to improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver your license key after purchase
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important product updates (you can opt out at any time)
  • Improve Axovera based on usage patterns

3. Data Storage

Your advertising metrics entered into Axovera are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the numbers you enter into the tool.

Your license key is stored locally in your browser's localStorage for convenience so you don't have to re-enter it on each visit.

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paddle — Payment processing. Subject to Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Website hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

Axovera uses minimal cookies and browser storage only for essential functionality such as storing your license key and theme preference. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@axovera.com.
